Welcome to Vaasa

Enjoy the West Coast of Finland

Fun things to do when you visit:

Experience the archipelago

Go for a sauna and a swim, rent a kayak and explore, or grab something to eat at one of the restaurants on the waterfront.

Go bike riding

Vaasa is a bike friendly town with lots of bike paths. If you're up for the challenge, there is a wide variety of forest trails to ride on.

Take a hike or something

If riding a bike isn't your thing, there are many trails that are nice to just walk on. Pack a small lunch and enjoy the calm away from the city.
